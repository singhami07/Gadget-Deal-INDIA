Frequently asked questions

How does DealGadget India find its deals?

We track live prices across 40+ Indian retailers including Amazon.in, Flipkart, Myntra, Nykaa and Croma, and compare each current price to its 30-day rolling average. Only genuine drops make it onto the site — inflated "was" prices are filtered out automatically.

Is DealGadget India free to use?

Yes, browsing and searching deals is completely free. We may earn a small affiliate commission when you buy through a listed link, at no extra cost to you — this is how we keep the tracking running.

How often are deals updated?

Prices refresh hourly for trending categories like mobiles and electronics, and at least twice a day for every other category on the site.

Can I get alerts for a specific product?

Yes — sign up with your email above and choose your categories; you'll get a daily digest of only the drops that match what you care about.
